FAQ
Why Odo?
Odo gives your users secure access to internal applications without the cost and hassel of managing VPNs. Odo expands secure access past traditional perimeter-based network security with the power to grant access to any application, to any user, from any device, while maintaining security hygiene.
With Odo you can…
  • Differentiate between corporate and personal devices.
  • Limit sensitive data access to only corporate devices.
  • Limit remote access to specific applications without exposing the network.
