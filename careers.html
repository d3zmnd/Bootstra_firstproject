DevOps Engineer ︳R&D Team
Odo is looking for an experienced DevOps Engineer to be responsible for build and maintain our cloud infrastructure.
Our elite team is fast, innovative and flexible; with individual ownership. We expect great things from our engineers and reward them with challenging projects, emerging technologies and the chance to be part of a company that is about to change the very foundation of remote network access.

The ideal candidate will share our passion and great pride in elegant solutions and will join an engineering team that strongly believes in designing for scale, rigorous code reviews, automated testing, and an iterative process to build high-quality next-generation enterprise software.
Apply
Responsibilities
  • Build and maintain the company's entire cloud high scale production infrastructure.
  • Ownership of the company’s CI/CD pipelines
  • Implementation of logging and monitoring
  • Automation of data flows
Required Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:
  • At least 3+ years experience as a DevOps/SRE, working with large scale cloud production environments
  • Hands on experience in working on at least one of the leading cloud platforms: AWS, GCP, Azure
  • Vast experience with CI/CD pipelines and automation
  • Experience in container orchestration - Kubernetes, Docker Swarm.
  • Proven ability in raising and maintaining logging & monitoring solution (ELK stack, Datadog)
  • Solid understanding in networking
  • Working knowledge with SQL or NoSQL datastores and messaging systems
  • Experience in “Infrastructure as code”. Preferred: Ansible, Terraform
  • Strong background with scripting languages like Bash, Python in Linux environment
  • Knowledge of HashiCorp suite (Consul, Vault, Packer…) a plus
Why You'll Love Odo:
  • We have a “no mean people” rule. Seriously.
  • You will have an opportunity to play a critical role in the design of our core product.
  • Vast experience with CI/CD pipelines and automation
  • You’ll have lots of room to grow. The only limit to your potential here is your own dedication to succeed.
  • You'll have the autonomy to make decisions that are best for the company.
What We're Building

Odo is fundamentally changing the way organizations design and operate their networks. Built for today’s mobile workforce, Odo’s zero-trust platform delivers trusted access to any application, from any device and any location, without the need for VPNs.

Some of the nicest and smartest people you will ever meet: Odo is well-funded, backed by the best, and bringing together great minds to do great things.
